Please create the following icon files for the extension:

1. icon16.png (16x16 pixels)
2. icon48.png (48x48 pixels)
3. icon128.png (128x128 pixels)

The icons should follow these guidelines:
- Use a shield or security-related symbol
- Use a modern, flat design style
- Primary color: #2196F3 (Blue)
- Secondary colors: #4CAF50 (Green) for safe status
- Accent colors: #FFC107 (Amber) for warnings, #F44336 (Red) for danger

The icons should be clear and recognizable even at small sizes.
